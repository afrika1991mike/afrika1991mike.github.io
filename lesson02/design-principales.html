Contrast

Youtub

Youtub.com

Youtub website is a good example of contrast principale. It has distinct difference between foreground and background colors in order to enhance readability. Contrast does not only deal with colors but also blends into visual hierarchy of font sizes,
image placement, and content.

Alignment

Amazon

Amazon.com

Amazon site is good example of alignment principale.It simply refers to how elements are placed on a page. Its purpose goes beyond organization, it is vital for enhancing the overall visual appeal of a design or page layout.
